Our mission is to advance the frontier of Science and Engineering of Materials, Mechanics, and Manufacturing!

The CEMAM is at the forefront of innovation, driving advancements in design, manufacturing, and testing to transform the future of engineering. Our research seamlessly integrates cutting-edge mechanics and material science with state-of-the-art manufacturing techniques, enabling the creation of high-performance, complex structures with unparalleled precision. Through collaborative efforts and multidisciplinary approaches, we are pushing the boundaries of what's possible in material design and production, empowering industries to achieve greater efficiency, sustainability, and functionality in their manufacturing processes. Join us as we shape the future of manufacturing technology.

Read our story

Our founding

Our founding was driven by a shared vision to revolutionize the intersection of mechanics, materials, and manufacturing through the power of additive manufacturing technologies. Established by a group of pioneering researchers and industry leaders, we aimed to create a hub of innovation that would propel the future of engineering and production.

Growth & beyond

Our growth has been fueled by a relentless commitment to pioneering research and innovation, with a vision to lead the global evolution of manufacturing technologies and redefine industry standards.
